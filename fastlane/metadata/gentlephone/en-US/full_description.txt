gentle phone: Connectivity Simplified.

gentle phone is the caregiver-supported variant of the popular "Simple Phone". It is designed specifically for seniors and anyone needing a simplified interface due to vision or dexterity challenges.

The Best of Both Worlds
You get the rock-solid reliability of Simple Phone's interface, plus a unique Caregiver Portal. This allows family members to remotely configure the phone—adjusting font sizes, managing display settings, or customizing the experience—from any web browser. No more confusing phone calls trying to explain which button to press!

Why Choose gentle phone?
*   Remote Caregiver Support: Settings can be managed remotely by a trusted contact.
*   See Clearly: Large fonts and high-contrast themes (Deep Blue / Bright Green).
*   Touch Easily: Massive buttons ensure you never miss a tap.
*   Stay Safe: Optional "Call Confirmation" prevents accidental dials.
*   Focus on What Matters: Smart "Photo Contacts" put loved ones one tap away.

Smart Features for Seniors:
*   Voice Announcements: Hear who you are calling before the call connects.
*   No Accidental Swipes: A simple, tap-based interface tailored for motor control challenges.
*   Privacy Focused: No ads, no tracking. Your data stays on your device.

Download gentle phone to restore the joy of simple communication.
