Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how gentle phone ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and the companion Caregiver Portal.

1. Data Collection and Usage

gentle phone is designed to be a private, senior-friendly dialer. We prioritize your privacy and minimize data collection.

We collect and use the following data:

  • Contacts: The App accesses your device's contacts solely to display them within the App and to facilitate making phone calls.
  • Call Log: The App accesses your call log to display recent calls and missed calls.
  • Phone State: The App accesses phone state to detect incoming calls and ongoing calls.
  • Device Settings: We store non-personal configuration settings (e.g., text size, dark mode preference, user display name) to synchronize your preferences across devices.

2. Internet Access and Data Sync

Unlike the standard version of Simple Phone, gentle phone uses an internet connection to provide remote management features for caregivers.

We use Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication) to securely sync configuration settings between the App and the Caregiver Portal. This allows caregivers to remotely assist with device setup.

No personal call data (audio, history, or contact details) is ever transmitted to our servers. Only configuration preferences are synced.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function as a phone dialer and sync settings:

  • Read/Write Contacts: To display and manage your contacts locally.
  • Call Phone: To initiate phone calls.
  • Read Call Log: To display your call history locally.
  • Read Phone State: To handle incoming and outgoing calls.
  • Internet: To synchronize configuration settings with the Caregiver Portal.
  • Default Dialer: To function as your primary phone application.

4. Authentication

The Caregiver Portal uses Google Sign-In to authenticate caregivers. We receive your basic profile information (name, email, and profile picture) to verify your identity and display your manager status on the connected device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at stv.heuscher@gmail.com.
